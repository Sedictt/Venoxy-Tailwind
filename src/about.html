About Me

Hi! I'm Venoxy, a Digital Artist & Developer with a passion for creativity across different mediums. I create digital and traditional drawings, bringing ideas to life through visual expression.

I'm particularly drawn to photography, especially street photography where I capture authentic moments in urban environments. As a programmer, I also develop web applications and create web designs that combine functionality with aesthetics. I'm constantly expanding my skill set and have recently started exploring animation with the goal of eventually delving into game development.

  • Philosophy: Creativity knows no bounds - I believe in exploring multiple disciplines to create unique and meaningful work
  • Notable Interests: Digital art, traditional drawing, street photography, web development, UI/UX design, animation, and game development
  • Fun Fact: I'm always learning new technologies and techniques, constantly pushing my creative boundaries
